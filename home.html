Our Mission

Our mission at Chadwear.com.au is to provide people with the wear that Chad would wear, at a reasonable price. The wear that Chad would wear can be summed up as being "casual", for all occasions. Looking smart and being formal for any event is what we strive to provide. As well as supporting the local community and providing to the people of the country, we donate to the charity FedoraFriends. Also, we strive to be an equal opportunity employer, without being biased towards any particular person or people based on sex, religion or beliefs.

Trends

We have always been a clothing provider. What started out as smart-casual wear turned into a business providing not only clothing of all types, but accessories too. All that we sell is suitable for any occasion, whtether is be smart, formal, casual, smart-casual, or Friday casual.

History

Chad and Aaron are the founders of this business, noticing a need for more casual clothes in, not only their lives, but the lives of the people they saw around them. They began Chadwear, with the goal to bring smarter, more casual clothes to the people. After a few years of success, they grew and expanded opening more stores, and stretching further into both ends of smart-casual. And here we are today, with a brand new website to commemmorate 10 years, and new stock.
